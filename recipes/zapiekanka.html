Description

Try this traditional polish street-food! It's a big hit in its origin country, will it be a one in your home as well?

Zapiekanka

Ingredients

  • Baguette
  • Mushrooms
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Fry mushrooms until soft, season well.
  2. Spread fried mushrooms on each half of the baguette and sprinkle them with grated cheese.
  3. Toast until cheese has melted.
